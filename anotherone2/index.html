For 40 years, Hawai‘i Foodbank has been synonymous with getting food in the hands of those in need.
We believe that consistent access to safe and healthy food is a fundamental human right, and the Foodbank
has been working to ensure that no one goes hungry in our communities for four decades now. Looking forward, we are
working to broaden our reach by building capacity, strengthening support to our partner networks, pursuing
advocacy and outreach opportunities, purchasing more food, and, most importantly, building connections.
